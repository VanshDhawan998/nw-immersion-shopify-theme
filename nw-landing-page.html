The Story That Sparked NW Immersion

NW Immersion began with a personal journey. In 2010, founders Michelle and Eric installed their first home sauna. What started as a wellness practice soon became a way of life. For Eric, the change was transformative.

Through regular sauna use, he lost 100 pounds and kept it off – a lasting reminder of the power of heat and cold immersion. Together, Michelle and Eric built NW Immersion on the belief that wellness should be accessible, enjoyable, and deeply rewarding.

Today, every sauna we build carries that same commitment: helping you discover health and renewal one session at a time.
